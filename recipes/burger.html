Below is the detailed recipe of Burger

Ingredients needed

Main Ingredients

  • 50 g minced meat( chicken or beef)
  • 2 burger bun
  • 1 small onion, very finely chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • Half tsp black pepper
  • Half tsp salt
  • Half tsp red chilli powder or paprika
  • One tsp oil or butter
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Ketchup

Optional

  • Cheese slices
  • Jalape√±os

Process

  1. In a bowl,mix minced meat, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, chilli powder
  2. Divide into two portions and shape round patties, Slightly flatten the center
  3. Heat a pan on medium heat, add oil or butter
  4. Cook patties three to four minutes per side until golden and cooked through
  5. Toast the buns

Assemble

  1. Bottom bun
  2. sauce
  3. lettuce
  4. patty
  5. tomato
  6. onion
  7. more sauce
  8. top bun

Hola! Burger is ready to serve and eat
