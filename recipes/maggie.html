Below is the detailed recipe of Maggie

Ingredients needed

Main Ingredients

  • 400 ml water
  • Maggie packet
  • Maggie Masala-comes with packet

Optional

  • Veggies
  • sliced potato

Process

  1. Boil water for two minutes
  2. Add Maagie nooddles to water
  3. Add Maggie Masala
  4. add Veggies
  5. Wait for two minutes

Hola! Maggie is ready to serve and eat
